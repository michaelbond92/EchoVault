# EchoVault Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 2024**

## Introduction

EchoVault ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

**EchoVault is a personal mental health journaling application. We take the privacy of your mental health data extremely seriously.**

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Journal Entries**: Text entries, voice recordings, and transcriptions you create
- **Account Information**: Email address and authentication data when you sign in with Google
- **Mood Data**: Mood ratings and emotional context you provide with entries
- **Safety Plans**: Personal safety information, coping strategies, and emergency contacts you choose to store

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage Data**: Features used, interaction patterns (stored locally and in your private Firebase account)
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version (for compatibility purposes only)

### Information Processed by Third-Party AI Services

To provide AI-powered features, certain data is processed by third-party services:

- **Voice Transcription**: Audio recordings are sent to OpenAI's Whisper API for transcription
- **Entry Analysis**: Journal entry text is sent to Google Gemini API for mood analysis and insights
- **Smart Search**: Entry embeddings are generated using AI services for semantic search

**Important**: We do not store your data on third-party AI servers beyond the time needed to process your request. These services process data according to their respective privacy policies.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and maintain the EchoVault service
- Transcribe your voice recordings into text
- Generate AI-powered insights and mood analysis
- Enable smart search across your journal entries
- Detect potential crisis situations and provide safety resources
- Improve and optimize the application

## Data Storage and Security

### Where Your Data Is Stored

- **Firebase Cloud Firestore**: Your journal entries, mood data, and account preferences are stored in Google Firebase, isolated to your user account
- **Local Device**: Some data may be cached locally for offline access

### Security Measures

- All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Firebase provides encryption at rest
- Authentication is handled securely through Google Sign-In
- Your data is isolated by your unique user ID

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do not sell your personal information.**

We may share your information only in these circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: With AI service providers (OpenAI, Google) solely to provide transcription and analysis features
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or to protect our rights
- **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly authorize sharing

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

- **Access**: View all your stored journal entries and data within the app
- **Delete**: Delete individual entries or your entire account
- **Export**: Export your journal entries (PDF export feature)
- **Opt-Out**: Stop using voice recording or AI features at any time

## Data Retention

- Your data is retained as long as you maintain an active account
- You can delete your data at any time through the app
- Upon account deletion, your data is permanently removed from our systems

## Children's Privacy

EchoVault is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

## Mental Health Disclaimer

EchoVault is a journaling tool and is **not a substitute for professional mental health care**. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis:

- **United States**: Call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)
- **Crisis Text Line**: Text HOME to 741741
- **Emergency**: Call 911 or your local emergency number
